Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Overview

Ignite Applications LLC ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes how our apps handle your information.

Data Collection

Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data, including saved mixes and preferences, is stored locally on your device and is never sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may include third-party advertising services in the future. If introduced, those services may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We will update this policy accordingly.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking tools. We have no access to your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
